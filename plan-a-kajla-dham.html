The Call of Kajla Dham

The KAJLA DHAM temple, located in Hisar, Haryana, is a significant religious site dedicated to Lord Hanuman. It is known for being a place of worship for many devotees, with a beautiful main temple of Lord Hanuman and various other deities, including Lord Ram's family, Lord Ganesha, Vishnu-Lakshmi, Goddess Durga, Shiva family, and Radha-Krishna.


The temple hosts several major festivals throughout the year, particularly Hanuman Jayanti, during which the temple is beautifully decorated. Facilities for the accommodation and meals of devotees are provided, ensuring they do not face any difficulties. The temple has been organizing community meals (Bhandara) for the last 35 years, along with five large fairs each year.


The spiritual atmosphere of the temple brings peace and satisfaction to visitors. Additionally, special Havan (fire rituals) and Bhajan (devotional singing) sessions are organized for the devotees. If you are planning to travel for the New Year, KAJLA DHAM is an excellent destination.

Important Information:

  • Location: Shri Ram Bhakt Hanuman Temple, Shri Kajla Dham, Hisar, Haryana 125052
  • Timing: 5:00 AM to 9:00 PM
  • Nearest Railway Station: Hisar Railway Station (approximately 20.3 kilometers away)
  • Nearest Airport: Indira Gandhi International Airport (approximately 178 kilometers away)

Devotees visiting here are sure to receive the blessings of Lord Hanuman.
